. Digital Couture
I believe brands should feel personal, I believe design should communicate without over explaining, I believe growth is more powerful than perfection and I also believe that creativity, individuality and data are not meant to compete but collaborate. That is what Digital Couture line is all about, it show cases all this.

As A Business Intelligence and Data Analyst student, a young business woman and aspiring data analyst I am fascinated by how numbers, clothes and personalities tell stories and how fashion design makes those stories visible. This gallery showcases the visual side of that journey.
